Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • WAEC/NECO with minimum 5 credits including Mathematics and English
  • Strong foundation in Science subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • JAMB score of 180 and above (subject to change)
  • Pass FUTMINNA Post-UTME examination
  • Minimum O'Level credit in relevant subjects

Documentation

  • Birth Certificate or National Identity Card
  • WAEC/NECO Certificate or Statement of Results
  • JAMB Registration Number and Result
  • Valid Email Address
  • Proof of Address
  • Recent Passport Photograph (4x6)

Entry Modes

Direct Entry

For candidates with National Diploma (ND) or equivalent qualifications. Direct admission to 200 level.

  • ND in relevant field
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5
  • JAMB score of 120+

UTME Entry

For candidates with Senior Secondary Certificate (SSCE). Join the regular 4-year program from 100 level.

  • SSCE with 5 credits
  • JAMB score of 180+
  • Pass Post-UTME exam

Postgraduate Entry

For candidates with Bachelor's degree. Pursue Master's and PhD programs in various disciplines.

  • Bachelor's degree (2nd class lower)
  • TOEFL/IELTS (if international)
  • Relevant GRE score

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the admission process start?

The admission process typically starts in January with JAMB registration and continues through the year. Check the Important Dates section for specific timelines.

What is the minimum JAMB score required?

The minimum JAMB score requirement is typically 180 and above, but this may vary yearly. Check the official FUTMINNA website for current requirements.

Can I change my program after admission?

Program change is possible during specific periods. Contact the Registrar's office for the exact procedure and deadlines.

What accommodation options are available?

FUTMINNA provides on-campus accommodation for students. Priority is given to freshmen and international students.

Are there scholarships available?

Yes, various scholarship schemes are available based on academic merit and financial need. Contact the Student Affairs office for details.

How do I contact the admissions office?

You can reach the admissions office via email at admissions@futminna.edu.ng or call +234 (0) 66 228 3806.
