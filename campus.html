Our Facilities

Central Library

Our state-of-the-art library houses over 150,000 books, digital resources, and study spaces. Features include:

  • Multiple reading sections
  • Computer labs with internet access
  • Digital archives and databases
  • Quiet study zones
  • 24/7 security

Advanced Laboratories

Over 100 specialized laboratories equipped with modern instruments for practical learning:

  • Physics & Chemistry labs
  • Computer science labs
  • Engineering workshops
  • Research centers
  • Simulation facilities

Residential Halls

Comfortable accommodation for students featuring modern amenities:

  • Well-furnished rooms
  • WiFi connectivity
  • 24-hour security
  • Cafeterias & canteens
  • Recreational areas

Sports Complex

Comprehensive sports facilities for recreation and competitive activities:

  • Football fields
  • Tennis courts
  • Basketball courts
  • Swimming pools
  • Gym & fitness center

Auditorium & Convention Center

World-class venue for conferences, seminars, and cultural events:

  • 5,000+ seating capacity
  • Advanced AV systems
  • Breakout rooms
  • Catering services
  • Modern acoustics

Health Center

Comprehensive healthcare services for students and staff:

  • Medical consultations
  • Emergency services
  • Pharmacy
  • Vaccination programs
  • Counseling services
